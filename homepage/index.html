Recent Expert Advice

How to control stem borer in rice crops?

Use neem-based pesticides or introduce natural predators like Trichogramma wasps. Avoid excessive nitrogen fertilization which makes plants more susceptible.
Answered by: Officer Rajesh Kumar 2 days ago

Best fertilizers for tomato farming in red soil?

For red soil, use a balanced NPK fertilizer (10-10-10) with added micronutrients like zinc and boron. Organic compost is highly recommended to improve soil structure.
Answered by: Officer Priya Sharma 5 days ago

When is the best time to plant wheat in northern regions?

The optimal window is between November 1-15 for most northern regions. Early sowing leads to excessive vegetative growth while late sowing reduces yield potential.
Answered by: Officer Ravi Singh 1 week ago
